About Combify

What it is. Combify is a round timer that calls out real boxing combinations out loud, so members can get solid, guided reps in whenever they're not in class — no partner, no pads, just a phone.

Why it exists. Members who train more between sessions improve faster and stick with it longer. Combify gives that home training real structure instead of aimless shadowboxing — and it's something built specifically for Boxing With Bakr, not a generic fitness app.

What's next. Combos, levels, and pacing are all easy to keep editing and improving as we learn what actually helps members train.
